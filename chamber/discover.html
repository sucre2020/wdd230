Tinapa Resort

Tinapa is a business and a leisure resort located in the of Calabar Muinicipality, Cross River State, Nigeria associated with the Calabar Free Trade Zone. It was developed in four phases under a private public Partnership promoted by the Cross River State.

Marina Resort in Calabar sits on a great historical site, the mouth of Calabar. It stands near old slave trade sites, where 30% of the slaves that left Africa were deported.

Agbokim-Waterfalls

Water Falls are situated in the Etung Local Government Area of Cross River State in the South-Eastern region of Nigeria very close to it's border with Cameroon. The waterfalls are about 15 kilometers from Ikom and 320 kilometers from Calabar.

Obudu Mountain Resort

Obudu Mountain Resort is a ranch and a resort on the Obudu plateau in Cross River State, Nigeria. Since 2005, a cable car climbing 870 meters from the base to the top of the plateau gives visitors a scenic view while bypassing the extremely winding road to the top.
