White Space and Clean Design

Drop Box

https://www.dropbox.com/:

Whitespace is the negative areas in any composition. It's the unmarked distance between different elements that gives visual breaks when viewers process design. Drop box Website uses white space by assigning each element with a name, title and white spaces around them.

Rule of Thirds

Digital Photography School

https://digital-photography-school.com/rule-of-thirds/

Digital photography is a great example of The rule of thirds. They make use of a compositional guideline that breaks an image down into thirds (both horizontally and vertically) so you have nine pieces and four gridlines.

Visual Hierachy

Heco

https://www.helloheco.com/

Heco uses Visual Hierachy brilliantly in the organization of the design elements on the page so that the eye is guided to consume each design element in the order of intended importance. Visual hierarchy can be created using: Color and contrast.
